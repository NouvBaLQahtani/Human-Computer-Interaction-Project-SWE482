# YSR: Manage Your Road Rage

This project is part of the Human-Computer Interaction course under my bachelor's degree in software engineering

## Introduction

YSR is an innovative Android mobile application designed to help users control their anger and stay calm while driving. Recognizing the adverse impact of road rage on both personal well-being and public safety, YSR offers a comprehensive solution to address this prevalent issue.

## Key Features

1. **Personalized Audio Content**:
   - YSR provides users with a curated selection of audio content, including Quranic verses, soothing music, and calming podcasts.
   - The app's algorithm analyzes the user's preferences and listening history to deliver personalized audio recommendations, tailored to their individual needs and mood.

2. **YSR Chatbot**:
   - The app features a voice-based chatbot that enables users to engage in conversations and play interactive voice games.
   - The chatbot acts as a companion, offering a distraction and an outlet for users to vent their frustrations during long traffic delays.

3. **Siri Integration**:
   - YSR seamlessly integrates with Siri, allowing users to search for and play their desired audio content using voice commands.
   - This feature provides a hands-free and intuitive way for users to access the app's resources while driving.

## Usage Scenarios

1. **Anger Management**:
   - During periods of intense traffic congestion or frustrating driving conditions, users can turn to YSR to access calming audio content and engage with the chatbot.
   - The personalized audio recommendations and interactive conversations help users regulate their emotions and maintain a composed state of mind.

2. **Long Commutes**:
   - For drivers facing lengthy commutes, YSR offers a solution to alleviate boredom and provide a sense of companionship during the journey.
   - The app's Siri integration allows users to access their preferred audio content without compromising safety or taking their eyes off the road.

3. **Stress Reduction**:
   - YSR can be used proactively by drivers to manage their stress levels and prevent the onset of road rage, even in normal driving conditions.
   - The app's ability to provide personalized audio content and a supportive chatbot experience helps users maintain a positive mindset and improve their overall driving experience
